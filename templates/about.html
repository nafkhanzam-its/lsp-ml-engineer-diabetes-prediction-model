About Our System

Advanced AI-powered diabetes risk prediction for early intervention

Project Overview

The Diabetes Risk Prediction System was developed for Klinik Sehat Sentosa to identify patients at high risk of developing diabetes. This machine learning-powered tool enables early intervention and preventive care programs.

Objective

Enable early diabetes detection through AI-powered risk assessment for preventive healthcare.

Target Users

Healthcare professionals and patients seeking diabetes risk screening.

Technical Implementation

Machine Learning Model
  • Algorithm: Gradient Boosting Classifier
  • Accuracy: 85%+ validation accuracy
  • Features: 8 medical parameters
  • Training Data: Pima Indians Diabetes Database
Technology Stack
  • Backend: Python, Flask
  • ML Libraries: Scikit-learn, Pandas, NumPy
  • Frontend: Bootstrap 5, JavaScript
  • Deployment: Docker, Cloud-ready

Medical Parameters

Our AI model analyzes the following medical parameters to assess diabetes risk:

Pregnancies

Number of times pregnant (relevant for female patients)

Glucose Level

Plasma glucose concentration (mg/dL)

Blood Pressure

Diastolic blood pressure (mm Hg)

Skin Thickness

Triceps skin fold thickness (mm)

Insulin Level

2-Hour serum insulin (mu U/ml)

BMI

Diabetes Pedigree Function

Genetic predisposition based on family history

Age

Patient age in years
